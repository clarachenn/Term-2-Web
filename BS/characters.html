Characters

About

There are currently 33 different characters in Brawl Stars. Each brawler has a different speed, health, attack, and super. Each brawler is in a category from mythics to epics to legendaries. Legendaries are the hardest to get and the most rare. They are only avaiable in Brawl Boxes.

How to Get a Brawler

Every person who plays the game starts with the same brawler, Shelly. When you earn trophies, you can unlock "Trophy Road Brawlers." These brawlers can only be earned when the necessary amount of trophies is earned. Next, you can get other brawlers from opening brawl boxes.

Upgrading Your Brawlers

As you play games, you earn tokens. These tokens can be used to unlock Brawl Boxes. When you open Brawl Boxes, you are likely to receive power points and coins. The power points go to your brawlers and you can use the coins to upgrade them when they have enough power points. The more you upgrade, the higher the power level your character will have and the stronger the character will become.

Star Powers

When your brawler is at the maximum power level (by upgrading), they can unlock Star Powers. Star Powers can be found in Brawl Boxes. Star Powers give your character a special ability. Each brawler has 2 possible Star Powers.
